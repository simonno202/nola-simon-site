Still here? Good. That means you're that person.

You signed up for this newsletter years ago. I never emailed you. That's on me.

In 2020 I thought the most valuable thing I could offer was what I'd pioneered: hybrid and remote work, before it had a name, before anyone thought it was possible. I rode that wave for years.

Then I did something dangerous.

I kept thinking.

George Michael is my blueprint for career reinvention. Burn the jacket. Blow up the jukebox. Hire the supermodels.

*Faith* just got remastered and released on vinyl. Everything old is new again. I don't even own speakers that work with my turntable anymore — streaming is my jam — but in George, we trust.

Same energy.

When you blow up the past you are left with what remains. And I've realized that I'm a researcher at heart. A strategic futurist.

The hybrid/remote chapter made me. It doesn't define me.
